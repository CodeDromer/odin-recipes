Description

The best burger recipe for summertime grilling! This juicy burger is jam-packed with
all kinds of stuff and has no tasteless bread crumbs. Serve on buns with your favorite condiments.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Egg
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no
    longer pink in the center.
